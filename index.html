Our Story.

Iglesia Alianza Cristiana y Misionera Ebenezer is a community of believers who come together to worship God and grow in our faith in Jesus Christ. Our mission is to glorify God by making disciples of Jesus through various means such as worship services, fellowship events, and Bible studies. We also strive to serve and reach out to our local community and the world, sharing the love and message of Jesus with others. Whether you are a long-time follower of Christ or just beginning your journey, you are welcome to join us as we seek to deepen our relationship with God and love and serve others.

The leadership of Iglesia Alianza Cristiana y Misionera Ebenezer is committed to providing its members with a safe and comfortable place to worship, learn, and connect. To that end, we are embarking on several exciting capital improvement projects aimed at modernizing our facilities and enhancing their functionality. These projects include upgrades to our electrical and plumbing systems, installation of security cameras, and a complete renovation of our sanctuary, including new lighting and audio-visual systems. These improvements will not only enhance the beauty of our church but will also serve to increase its overall value and ensure that it remains a vibrant center of spiritual life for years to come.

Our Mission.

Our mission at Iglesia Alianza Cristiana y Misionera Ebenezer is to glorify God by making disciples of Jesus Christ, who love God and love others.

Our activities are all focused on achieving this mission by providing opportunities for believers to grow in their relationship with God, through worship, Bible study, and fellowship. Our worship services aim to bring glory to God and draw attendees closer to Him through uplifting music, biblical preaching, and meaningful prayer. Our Bible studies offer in-depth exploration of God's word and its application to our lives. Our fellowship events, such as small groups and retreats, provide a supportive community for believers to connect with each other and deepen their relationships with God and one another.

Additionally, we also prioritize serving and reaching out to our local community and the world, reflecting the love and compassion of Jesus to those around us. Whether through acts of kindness, volunteering, or local and global missions, our goal is to demonstrate the transformative power of Jesus and share His message with those who may not yet know Him.

In all of these activities, our ultimate objective is to bring glory to God and help others come to know and love Him more deeply.
